What does a Data Analyst do?

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. A data analyst plays a critical role in assisting organizations to make well-informed decisions by interpreting and analyzing data to find valuable insights. Their job begins by collecting data from diverse sources such as databases, surveys, web scraping tools, and APIs. Once gathered, the data undergoes a cleaning process, eliminating errors, inconsistencies, and missing values to ensure accuracy before any analysis takes place. The next step involves exploring the data using statistical techniques, identifying patterns, correlations, outliers, and trends that can provide valuable understanding. To communicate findings, data analysts create visuals like charts, graphs, and dashboards, making complex insights easy for stakeholders to understand.
